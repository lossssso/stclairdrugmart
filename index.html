Welcome to Our Pharmacy

St. Clair Drug Mart Pharmacy has been proudly serving the St. Clair West community, providing personalized pharmacy care you can count on. We're more than just a drugstore — we're your health partners right in the neighbourhood.

From prescription dispensing and blister packaging to travel vaccines and medication reviews, our licensed pharmacists are here to answer your questions and make sure you get the right care, every time.

Walk in, call us at (416) 654-8181, or book online — we're always happy to help.

New to Our Pharmacy?

Switching from another pharmacy or chain is easier than you think — we handle everything.

1

Transfer Your Prescription

Use our online transfer form below, call us at (416) 654-8181, or just bring your medication bottle in. We contact your current pharmacy — you don't have to do a thing.

2

We Set Up Your File

Our pharmacist reviews your full medication list, checks for interactions, and sets up your profile. Most transfers are ready the same day, with no gap in your medications.

3

Pick Up & Meet Us

Come in and meet your pharmacist in person, or choose free local delivery. No paperwork, no extra cost — just better, more personal care from your new neighbourhood pharmacy.
