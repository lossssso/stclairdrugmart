Welcome to Our Pharmacy

St. Clair Drug Mart Pharmacy has been proudly serving the St. Clair West community, providing personalized pharmacy care you can count on. We're more than just a drugstore — we're your health partners right in the neighbourhood.

From prescription dispensing and blister packaging to travel vaccines and MedsCheck reviews, our licensed pharmacists are here to answer your questions and make sure you get the right care, every time.

Walk in, call us at (416) 654-8181, or book online — we're always happy to help.

About Us

St. Clair Drug Mart Pharmacy is an independent community pharmacy proudly serving the St. Clair West neighbourhood in Toronto. We believe in building real relationships with our patients — taking time to listen, explain, and ensure every person leaves with the information and medication they need to thrive.

As an independent pharmacy, every decision we make is guided by what's best for our community, not a corporate bottom line. Come in and experience the difference.
